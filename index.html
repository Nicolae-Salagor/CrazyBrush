Exterior Painting

Save Time Money and Get Your Home Looking Amazing

We provide exterior painting services for residential and commercial properties across greater Ireland. The difference between painting the inside of a property and the outside of a property is that the outside is exposed to the changing elements. This means that the problem is getting the completed work to last for as long as possible. We prepare the external surfaces by :

    Removal of old paint

    Sanding the areas to be worked on

    Preparation and filling of cracks

    Preparation to ensure smooth surfaces
